  <action>
Present the following verification checklist to the user. The user must open http://localhost:5173 in a browser and test each item:

1. PLAY-01 + MOBL-01: Video fills the entire screen vertically with no letterboxing. No white bars above/below. Bottom controls are not clipped by browser chrome.

6. PLAY-06: A thin white progress bar at the very bottom edge advances during playback and resets on loop.
7. MOBL-03: Mute button is easy to tap with a thumb (at least 44px touch target).
8. MOBL-04: Only one video is playing (trivially true in single-video Phase 2).

For mobile testing (optional but recommended):
